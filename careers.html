We are always looking for talented new individuals to join our ranks.

Available Positions

 
 

  • We see every day as a chance to create a positive impact. We lead through our values centred on our team, diversity, equality and inclusion. We are looking for Strategic Opportunity Partners in the Startups, Innovation, and Technology industries, with a growth mindset and hunger to take ownership of local, national, and international opportunities through relationship building and fostering of strategic alliances.

    Our Strategic Opportunity Partners are on-demand, contract and referral based positions. Through Moken, Strategic Opportunity Partners (SOPs) have the ability to identify, procure, and source clients and opportunities on the schedule, frequency, and as tenaciously or sporadically as they choose and that suits their lifestyle.

    Strategic Opportunity Partners source deals through building global relationships and partnerships with individuals, startups, enterprises, and organizations to identify various opportunities for need fulfilment. SOPs then work collaboratively with their Moken team members and counterparts to fulfil the needs of the client through the liaison of transactional activities.

    In a nutshell, Strategic Opportunity Partners are responsible for sourcing procurement deals throughout a variety of sectors on a local, national, and international scope.
